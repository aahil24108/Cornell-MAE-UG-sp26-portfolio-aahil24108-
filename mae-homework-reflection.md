## Selected Assignment
**Mechanics of Materials (MAE 3270) Homework Reflection**

## Reflection
The homework assignment I found most valuable in this course was a Mechanics of Materials assignment because it helped me strengthen both my technical understanding and my overall approach to engineering problem solving. While completing the assignment, I had to carefully apply course concepts step by step and make sure my assumptions, calculations, and units were all consistent. This process taught me that solving mechanical engineering problems is not just about getting the final answer, but about building a logical solution that is organized, accurate, and clearly supported by engineering principles. It also helped me become more confident in working through multi-step problems in a structured way.

This assignment will be useful to me in future MAE coursework and in professional engineering settings because it improved both my analytical thinking and my technical communication skills. I learned the importance of clearly explaining each step of a solution so that someone else can follow my reasoning. That is an important skill in engineering design, analysis, and teamwork, where solutions often need to be justified and communicated clearly. Overall, this assignment helped me build a stronger foundation in careful analysis, attention to detail, and problem-solving, which will continue to be valuable throughout my academic and professional career.
